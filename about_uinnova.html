About uinnova

"uinnova Technology is a the leader in I.T Visualization and Data Visualization.

The uinnova team is composed of specialist specializing in computer diagram and I.T Management software. Uinnova's goal is to 'Help humanity better understand and manage the new world'. Focusing on smart strategic decisions, technical strength and logical cross industry ideas, uinnova has grown rapidly, already becoming China's fastest growing I.T Management software company.

Tarsier, a full series of IT Visualization software product created by uinnova, allows users to comprehensively visualize data centers and IT data by visualizing physical objects virtually, visualizing logic as infrastructure, visualizing from a global perspective to specific ports and visualizing monitored data from configurations. uinnova uses the most advanced 3D and 2D drawing technology and data processing technology to process, visualize and manage I.T related management data from different departments and tools. Uinnova has provided Data centers an innovative way to visually manage their services and help develop solutions for many IT infrastructure and Data Center management problems.

uinnova's IT Visualization product was well received by the market upon it's successful release and has been used by many of China's top industry I.T departments and their data centers such as ICBC, China Construction Bank, China Merchants Bank, PICC, China Mobile, China Unicom, SINOPEC, CNPC, China Shenhua, government groups, State Intellectual Property Office, China Academy of Sciences etc. In the December of 2013, uinnova was awarded the 'Futuristic Idea and Design Theory' Award by DataCenterDynamics, the only software product that was awarded throughout the event.

In 2016, after the foundational success of I.T visualization products, uinnova released it's very own I.O.T Visualization platform, for smart buildings, smart campuses and smart cities, providing these industries with I.O.T 3D visualiztion solutions. At the same time, uinnova has started applying it's solutions to other industries and professions, building a path towards 'Visualizing Everything'.

About Momoda

Momoda is an IoT PaaS platform created by uinnova, providing an easy to use visualization tool for IoT users.

By using Momoda, users can quickly build 3D visualization scenes. These scenes include complex interior and exterior designs of rooms, buildings and campus areas and are commonly used by schools, hospitals, marketplaces, factories and similar settings.

Scenes created using Momoda can be used in many different ways including:

  • 3D Design Pictures: More accurate and direct results for designs
  • 3D Sandbox: 3D Interactive sandbox, an innovative way to display campuses and buildings
  • Interactive Navigation: Dynamic guiding routes for campuses and large buildings

Scenes generated using Momoda can be used as a management method where IoT conditions can be met, or be combined with an IoT management system, acting as a 3D visualization GUI. Using IoT management API provided by uinnova, users can connect their 3D scene with an IoT management system to transfer data in real time, displaying statuses, events, alerts within your 3D scene and even allowing users to control these devices. Through using Momoda users can more directly visualize, control and manage the world of IoT allowing them to more effectively manage in fields of smart architecture, smart campuses and smart cities.

Momoda is uinnova's first and most important step from 'Visualize I.T' to 'Visualize Everything'. Uinnova has proven to be successful in the most complicated and sophisticated data centers, accumulating important experience during the process. Starting from Momoda, uinnova will use it's experience from data centers and I.T management to extend into new and more extensive IoT management fields, helping people better understand the era of IoT and managing the new world.
